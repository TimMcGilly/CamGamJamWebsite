About

Cambridge Game Jam 2023 is a 48 Hour Game Jam hosted in the William Gates Building (Computer Laboratory) by the Cambridge University student committee CS3.

You will have 48 hours to build a game in teams or individually based around a given theme that will be announced at the beginning of the jam!

Rules

Entry rules

  • Participants must be university students, academic staff, or under-18 non-students accompanied by a guardian.
  • Team sizes are limited to 5 people - if your team size is greater than that, we will split you into smaller groups.

Submission rules

  • Submissions will be made through the online service itch.io.
  • All submissions to the Jam must follow our Code of Conduct.
  • You retain all rights to any games and other content you make during the jam.
  • It is in the spirit of the jam to make the majority of code and assets you are using in your game during the jam however you can use any assets you are legally allowed to use as long as you credit it correctly in your submission.
  • Submissions must support Windows 10/11 or Web at minimum.
  • Submissions must not require additional software to be installed in order to run.
  • You may submit compressed .zip/.rar/.7zip file if the submission is too big for itch.io (itch.io has a 1GB limit)

FAQ

What is a Game Jam?

A game jam is a game building competition. You will be given a theme, and individually or in teams, you have to create a game from scratch within the time limit (48 hours in this case).

Is food provided?

Yes! Meals and snacks will be provided throughout!

Who can join?

Students, academic staff and under-18 non-students with a guardian attending and filling in their form for them.

How do I sign up?

Fill in the sign up form by 23:59 February 5th.

What size team can I be in?

Your group must be less or equal to than five people.

Where is it?

The game jam is located in the Intel lab of the William Gates Building. The address of which is 15 JJ Thomson Avenue, Cambridge, CB3 0FD, UK.

I don't want to work on my own, but don't know how to find a team

Join our discord server! https://discord.gg/fuam7P3yXn to find a team yourself or indicate on our form for us to assign you one randomly!

What should I bring?

Bring yourself, a water bottle and a laptop - that's all!
