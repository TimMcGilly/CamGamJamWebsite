FAQ

What is a Game Jam?

A game jam is a game building competition. You will be given a theme, and individually or in teams, you have to create a game from scratch within the time limit (48 hours in this case)

Who can join?

Students, academic staff and under-18 non-students with a guardian attending and filling in their form for them

How do I sign up?

fill in [form] by 23:59 February 5th

Where is it?

The game jam is located in the Intel lab of the William Gates Building. The address of which is 15 JJ Thomson Avenue, Cambridge, CB3 0FD, UK

I don't want to work on my own, but don't know how to find a team

Join our discord server! https://discord.gg/fuam7P3yXn to find a team yourself or indicate on our form for us to assign you one randomly!

What should I bring?

Bring yourself, a water bottle and a laptop - that's all!
